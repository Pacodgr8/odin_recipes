smoothies

10 minutes prep time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

How to make smoothies

  1. Step 1 Pour pineapple juice into a blender and add banana, strawberries, and blueberries. Cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
